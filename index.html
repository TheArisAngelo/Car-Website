about me

In a world where automotive culture is ever-evolving, there are those dare to defy the status quo and carve their own path. Leading the pack we find Akira Nakai, a name synonymous with tireless passion, breaking rules, and the relentess pursuit of humble perfection.

Throughout his twenties, Akira Nakai was immersed in the world of tuner and drift culture. Nakai-san was part of a crew that pushed the boundaries of racing with the iconic Toyota AE86s.

Today an RWB build by Nakai overseas often turns into an entertaining event. As he doesn't mind spectators during his process, many enthusiasts often gather at shops to see the master at work. Honestly, we highly advise you to dive into and RWB build Youtube rabbit hole, even if you're not a car head, they're fascinating to watch.
